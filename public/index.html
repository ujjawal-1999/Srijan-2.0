About the Event

“SRiJaN 1.0” is the first version of National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Summit organised by E Cell NIT Silchar. It is a unique initiative to motivate and educate people about entrepreneurship and to provide a meeting ground for Corporate experts and young budding entrepreneurs from NIT Silchar as well as several distinguished institutions across the country.

Organized by the Institute Innovation Council & Start-up Centre, NIT SILCHAR jointly with IEDC NIT Silchar, E-Cell NIT Silchar

About NIT SILCHAR

National Institute Of Technology, Silchar (राष्ट्रीय प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान, सिलचर) is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology of India and was established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College in Assam. In 2002 it was upgraded to the status of National Institute of Technology and was declared as Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

Outwit the Board Room

We understand it’s difficult to come up with an idea, but an idea is not all you need. Put your analytic skills to the test and improve on a pre-existing idea. On being allotted a well-established business, tune in with your team and discuss strategies to outsmart their ideas. Find ways to avoid making the same mistakes, and develop strategies to one-up their ideas.

Objective/Outcomes

The event forces the team to research and dive deep into the business model of a given company, tests their analytical observations and promotes critical thinking and teamwork. It also promotes creativity and working well within guidelines, which are important skills as an entrepreneur. Most of all, it promotes a spirit of improvement and learning based on others' experiences. It promotes a better understanding of finances, and promotes decision making under the pressure of knowing those decisions that affect people beyond their comprehension.

Business Model Canvas Competition

The Business Model Canvas gives people a common language through which they can evaluate traditional processes and bring innovation into their business models. It is how you ‘paint’ your business ideas as creatively as possible. So, come on down and show us your virtuosity and improve upon your idea through feedback of our business experts!

Rules

1. One participant from each team will be presenting the Green Canvas Model of their prospective ideas.

2. Value Proposition, Technology/method unique to your idea, Go-To market plan, Competitive Analysis and Financial Projections would be the basis of the judgement.

3. Panel’s decision would be final.

Board Room

Entrepreneurship is a multifaceted branch of management, which involves interpersonal communication of the highest command. A board room is a formal group discussion among ecstatic entrepreneurs, which is judged by our esteemed group of panellists and business experts. Their comments, not only help in improving communication skills but also gain some insight into business tactics.

Rules

1. One participant from each team will join the allotted board for the respective discussion.

2. The issues will be provided in hard copies to all the participants an hour before the commencement.

3. Judgement will be based on the preparedness of the participants, the details with which they’ll present their opinions, and the validity of the opinions.

IPL Auction

Ever imagined what it would be like to be a part of a live auction? Does the idea of placing bids and winning them in the heat of the moment against other bidders sound exciting? Then you’ve come to the right place. Welcome to the IPL Auction, an event under the E-Summit 2019: Srijan1.0 where you can put yourself in the shoes of a bidder to build your dream team from scratch to lift the IPL trophy with. Spend your allotted money wisely to pick your team from the basket of players provided in the rush hour of nerves, strategy and gut against other bidders with the aim of having the maximum number of points. If you love cricket, have followed IPL and can form a strategy for your bidding, then this event is just the right thing for you.

Rules


Prelims:

1. 12 Objective Questions related to IPL will be asked.

2. Top 5 teams will be selected for Final Round.

3. The team can have maximum of four members.

4. Time duration for this round will be 15 minutes.


Mains:

1. The bidding round will start after a break of 10 min post the prelim round.

2. The team which will not fulfil the conditions(to be announced soon) will be eliminated.

3. The team can have maximum of four members.

4. The team which will have maximum points at the end will win the round.
