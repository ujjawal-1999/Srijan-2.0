About the Event

“SRiJaN 2.0” is the National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Summit organised by E Cell NIT Silchar. It is a unique initiative to motivate and educate people about entrepreneurship and to provide a meeting ground for Corporate experts and young budding entrepreneurs from NIT Silchar as well as several distinguished institutions across the country.

Organized by the Institute Innovation Council & Start-up Centre, NIT SILCHAR in association with IEEE ComSoc NITS Student Branch Chapter

About NIT SILCHAR

National Institute Of Technology, Silchar (राष्ट्रीय प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान, सिलचर) is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology of India and was established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College in Assam. In 2002 it was upgraded to the status of National Institute of Technology and was declared as Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

Outwit the Board Room

We understand it’s difficult to come up with an idea, but an idea is not all you need. Put your analytic skills to the test and improve on a pre-existing idea. On being allotted a well-established business, tune in with your team and discuss strategies to outsmart their ideas. Find ways to avoid making the same mistakes, and develop strategies to one-up their ideas.

Objective/Outcomes

The event forces the team to research and dive deep into the business model of a given company, tests their analytical observations and promotes critical thinking and teamwork. It also promotes creativity and working well within guidelines, which are important skills as an entrepreneur. Most of all, it promotes a spirit of improvement and learning based on others' experiences. It promotes a better understanding of finances, and promotes decision making under the pressure of knowing those decisions that affect people beyond their comprehension.

Panel Discussion

Having a brilliant mind is great but having the ability of learning from the problems of experienced ones is the best habit one can nurture. Here we are with "Panel Discussion'' where real CEO, COO, CFO of various startups and companies will discuss the problem faced by them in running and raising a firm and they will also share some secret mantras of tackling such tough situations.Times is definitely money but experience is the eternal treasure.

Objective/Outcomes

Panel Discussions are far more fruitful compared to a speaker session. Seeing great minds interact and share their thought process gives an insight into their expertise. It is very lucrative for a budding entrepreneur to have their doubts discussed and cleared by a panel of experts.

Pitch Please

The contest aims to promote innovation-driven solutions to solve various challenges. Something as simple and small-scale initiative as their summer project or a research endeavour, come up with your ideas and pitch it in front of the panel of experts and let them examine your ability to evaluate the market, customer needs, product usability. All you need is, to participate in teams consisting of not more than 3 members including the team leader, come up with a business plan which should not only be profitable but also be feasible in the real world and pitch your idea in front of the Panel.

Rules

For stage 1(Online Screening)

Registered teams are supposed to submit a PPT about their prospective business plan/idea (in not more 20 slides). The PPT should highlight the sectors of people who will be benefited from the business and also prove the scalability and the profitability of the idea/plan. Deadline:- 11:59 PM, 07 April 2021

IPL Auction

The Trill, the suspense, the bids, and the AUCTION!! Build your dream team and your passion to form the best out of your favorite players. Bring out your craze for cricket and set your bidding strategies with your mates.

Rules For

Prelims

  • This round will consist of objective questions related to IPL.
  • 30 objective questions will be there.
  • Each team may have 3-5 members
  • Time duration will be 20 mins.
  • The top 5 teams will be selected for the final round.
  • Submission time will be considered as a tie-breaker.

Mains

  • The bidding round will be held amongst the top 5 teams of the previous round.
  • Each team will be provided a budget of 40Cr, using which they have to form a team of a maximum of 12 players.
  • The team that doesn’t fulfill the above conditions will be eliminated.
  • There will be a total of 80 players in the auction and each player will have points associated with him.
  • The team having maximum points by the end of the auction will win the round.

Digital Marketing

If you’re wondering what digital marketing is, it’s not just building network assets like Facebook Pages, Instagram Accounts, YouTube brand channels, blogs, websites etc., just because everyone seems to be doing so. Unplanned strategy or improper execution consequences into a collection of disconnected things that dispense or disorient the brand and confuse the clientele. As an entrepreneur or an aspiring digital marketer, it is essential that before you execute anything, you strategize, integrate and well- coordinate all offline and online assets.

This workshop will help you keep your brand’s identity intact as customers move across different platforms and help you ensure that each platform contributes incrementally to the brand’s value.

You will have a clear understanding of the tools, techniques and the knowledge required to develop proper marketing strategies and execute effective marketing campaigns.

Cyber Security Workshop

Cyber security is the act of safeguarding protecting data, networks, programs and other information from malicious attacks.

The cybersecurity industry is rapidly growing every day, with new technological developments comes new threats. Although more resources are being deployed to counter cyber-attacks, the nature of the industry still has a long way to go before we can, as a whole, catch up with these threats.

Here are some Alarming Cyber Security Facts and Stats:

  • There is a hacker attack every 39 seconds.
  • On average, only 5% of companies’ folders are properly protected.
  • Since COVID-19, the US FBI reported a 300% increase in reported cybercrimes
  • Approximately $6 trillion is expected to be spent globally on cybersecurity by 2021


Do you feel vulnerable that your data may go in wrong hands?

Here at SRIJAN 2.0 the cyber security workshop we will assist you on how to save yourself from various types of cyber threats & will also act as a stepping stone to an exciting career in Cyber Security.
